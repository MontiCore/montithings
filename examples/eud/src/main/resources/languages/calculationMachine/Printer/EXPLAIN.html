PrinterLang

This language specifies how the result is displayed.
Example 1:
    var: X
    style: Full
Example 2:
    var: Y
    style: Result

Here example 1 will print the entire calculation whit 'X' as variable name, while example 2 will just prints the resulting value.

Rules:
  • There must only exist the two lines 'var:' and 'style:'
  • The variable may be any string, that does not contain a space
  • There are two choosable styles: Full and Result
